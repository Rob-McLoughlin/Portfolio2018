About

Hey, I’m Rob! I’m a 22-year-old designer and developer from Dublin, Ireland. I work at Human, where I design and develop interfaces for the web. I am also finishing up my psychology degree in the Institute of Art Design and Technology in Dun Laoghaire, Dublin. A lot of my research is in the HCI and cognitive fields, looking at how we can use cognitive theory to design better interaction experiences.

I fell in love with design in the second year of my degree and started reading as much as I could about design. Always a fan of building things, I have continued to teach myself some languages that would help me develop for the web. What was my hobby has turned into my work, yet it is still something I do in my free time. I try to develop my skills further through working on projects outside of college and work.

You can email me at rob@robmcloughlindesign.com or find me on Twitter, LinkedIn or Instagram.
